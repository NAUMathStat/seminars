During the Spring 2021 semester, the ACGT, AMS, and FAMUS seminars will not take place. However, we will attempt to hold our weekly colloquium on Thursdays at 4-5pm via Zoom. Please contact Nandor Sieben to obtain the Zoom information or to be added to the mailing list that includes the Zoom information. A comprehensive and current list of online mathematics seminars can be found here.

Typically, the Mathematics and Statistics Department at NAU has four weekly seminars, but with the except on the Departmental Colloquium, the seminars are on hold until the pandemic subsides.

  • Departmental Colloquium on Tuesdays from 4:00-5:00pm with refreshments available from 3:45-4:00pm in Adel 164.
  • ACGT (Algebra, Combinatorics, Geometry, Topology) Seminar on Tuesdays from 1:00-1:50pm in Adel 221.
  • AMS (Applied Math Seminar) on Thursdays from 12:45-2:00pm in Adel 162.
  • FAMUS (Friday Afternoon Undergraduate Mathematics Seminar) on Fridays from 3:00-4:15pm with refreshments in Adel 164.
