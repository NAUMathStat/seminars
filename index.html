The Mathematics and Statistics Department at NAU has four weekly seminars.

  • Departmental Colloquium on Tuesdays from 4:00-4:50pm with refreshments available from 3:45-4:00pm in Adel 164.
  • ACGT (Algebra, Combinatorics, Geometry, Topology) Seminar on Tuesdays from 12:40-1:30pm in Adel 164.
  • AMS (Applied Math Seminar) on Thursdays from 12:40-1:30pm in Adel 224.
  • FAMUS (Friday Afternoon Undergraduate Mathematics Seminar) on Fridays from 3:00-4:00pm with refreshments in Adel 164.
